Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

HomeWorth ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. HomeWorth stores all data locally on your device. Your financial information — including asset values, account balances, and net worth calculations — never leaves your device.

We do not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit your financial data to our servers
  • Use analytics tools that track your personal information
  • Share your data with any third parties
  • Display advertisements based on your financial data

Local Data Storage

All asset and net worth data you enter in HomeWorth is stored locally on your iPhone using iOS's standard data storage. This data is not synced to our servers. If you uninstall the app, your data will be deleted.

Subscriptions

HomeWorth offers optional Pro subscriptions via App Store In-App Purchase. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. Please review Apple's privacy policy for details on payment data handling.

Notifications

If you enable weekly reminders, HomeWorth uses local notifications on your device. We do not send push notifications or collect notification data.

Children's Privacy

HomeWorth is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated revision date.
